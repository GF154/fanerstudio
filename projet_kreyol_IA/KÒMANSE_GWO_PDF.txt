3️⃣ ITILIZE API (Advanced):

   Standard Endpoint:
   ─────────────────
   POST http://localhost:8000/api/audiobook
   
   Form Data:
   • file: [ou fichye PDF]
   • voice: creole-native
   • max_pages: 500          ← NOUVO! (optional)
   • show_progress: true     ← NOUVO! (optional)
   
   
   Streaming Endpoint (pou PDF trè gwo):
   ──────────────────────────────────────
   POST http://localhost:8000/api/audiobook-streaming
   
   Form Data:
   • file: [ou fichye PDF]
   • voice: creole-native
   • chunk_size_pages: 100   ← NOUVO!
